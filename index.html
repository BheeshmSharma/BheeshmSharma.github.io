About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Industrial Engineering and Operations Research (IEOR) at IIT Bombay, working under the guidance of Prof. Balamurugan Palaniappan. My research is centered on developing efficient deep learning and multi-modal generative AI methods for applications in medical imaging as well as real-world image and video generation, with a special emphasis on few-shot, weakly supervised, and self-supervised learning paradigms.
